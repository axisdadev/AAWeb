What is PTFS?

Pilot Training Flight Simulator (PTFS) is a popular Roblox game where players can fly commercial, military, and private aircraft in a massive multiplayer world. We’re a virtual airline operating within PTFS, simulating real-world American Airlines flights on a weekly schedule, all inside a fun, community driven Roblox experience. Most of our activity, announcements, and flights happen in our Discord server, where passengers and crew hang out, chat, and stay updated.
